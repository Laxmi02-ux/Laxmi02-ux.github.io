## Projects

### 01 · Telecom Customer Churn Prediction
`Python` `SQL` `Power BI` `Logistic Regression` `Scikit-learn`

**The problem:** Telecom providers were losing their highest-value customers silently — no early-warning system existed.

**Key finding:** Month-to-month contracts churn at **42.7%** vs **2.8%** for 2-year plans. Churned customers pay 21% higher monthly charges — the most revenue-generating customers are the highest-risk.

**Delivered:** Full ML pipeline on 7,032 records → Logistic Regression model (78.89% accuracy, F1: 0.79, Recall: 0.81) + 3-page Power BI dashboard with contract-type and tenure filters. Exportable churn-risk list directly usable by a CRM or retention team.

| M2M vs 2-Year Churn | 42.7% vs 2.8% |

### 02 · Credit Risk & Loan Default Analysis
`Python` `SQL` `SQLite` `Power BI` `DAX`

**The problem:** A $312M loan portfolio had no segment-level default visibility — approval decisions relied on aggregate credit scores that masked behavioral risk patterns.

**Key finding:** Credit score alone is a weak predictor. **Loan grade, income-to-loan ratio (LTI), and credit history length** outperform it. LTI > 4x correlates with a **35% default rate**. 23% of the portfolio flagged as high-risk.

**Delivered:** End-to-end SQLite pipeline (ingestion → cleaning → segmentation → KPIs) + Power BI dashboard deployable by any credit risk or MIS team with zero additional setup.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Portfolio Size | $312M |
| Total Applicants | 32,581 |
| High-Risk (LTI > 4x) Default Rate | 35% |
| Portfolio Flagged High-Risk | 23% |

### 03 · Multi-Year Financial Performance Dashboard
`Python` `SQL` `Power BI` `DAX` `Excel`

**The problem:** Comparing 14 global companies across 8 sectors over 15 years manually is analyst-hours wasted — and error-prone. No scalable system existed to surface cross-sector stress signals.

**Key finding:** Leveraged sectors showed **diverging risk trajectories post-2016** — a stress signal only visible in multi-year cross-sector view. Built a custom **Financial Strength Index (FSI)**: `ROE×0.30 + Net Margin×0.30 + Current Ratio×0.20 − D/E×0.20`.

**Delivered:** 3-page Power BI report — revenue trends (2009–2023), company performance deep-dive with interactive slicers, and a risk & cash flow intelligence page with leverage vs. profitability scatter. Refreshable with new data in minutes.

| KPIs Tracked | 23 |
| Average ROE | 12.46% |

### 04 · Global Superstore: Executive Sales & Profit Intelligence
`SQL` `Power BI` `Excel` `DAX`

**The problem:** Management tracked top-line sales ($2.3M) but had no visibility into where margin was quietly being destroyed by discount strategy, category, or region.

**Key finding:** **$92K in recoverable loss** identified through discount-depth segmentation. Specific subcategories were discounted beyond the margin threshold — generating revenue while destroying profit. Technology leads with 36.4% of total sales.

**Delivered:** Executive dashboard with drill-down by region, sub-category, and discount tier across 5,009 orders (2014–2017). Geographical analysis via Bing Maps integration. Actionable by pricing team immediately.
